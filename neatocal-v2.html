If you print this page, you'll get a nifty calendar that displays all of the year's dates on a single page in a continuous weekly grid. It will automatically fit on a single sheet of paper of any size. For best results, adjust your print settings to portrait orientation and disable the header and footer.

Take in the year all at once. Fold it up and carry it with you. Jot down your notes on it. Plan things out and observe the passage of time. Above all else, be kind to others.

You can add URL parameters to change the year, display style, start month, etc.

  • year
  • start_month
  • n_month
  • layout (`continuous-weeks` is the default here)
  • highlight_color
  • weekday_code
  • month_code
  • show_moon_phase (`true` or `false`)
  • moon_phase_style (`css`, `symbol`, or `name`)
  • moon_phase_position (`below` or `inline`)
  • moon_phase_display (`changes` or `all`)
  • help
For example `?year=2030`
